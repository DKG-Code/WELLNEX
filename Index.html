Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 19, 2025

Welcome to WellMate. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our wellness app. By using WellMate, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: We use Firebase Authentication to collect your email or phone number for secure login.
  • Device Sensors: We collect step count and sleep activity using your phone's accelerometer for wellness tracking.
  • Food Logging: We store your meal entries including calories, proteins, fats, and carbs.
  • AI Interactions: We use Google Gemini Flash API to power your AI friend chat experience.
  • App Usage Data: We track your interactions with the app such as task completions, streaks, and activity graphs.
  • Health Indicators: Data used to generate Energy, Calm, Focus, and HP indicators is locally computed and optionally synced securely to Firebase.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To personalize your experience and health indicators.
  • To display streaks, goals, and progress graphs.
  • To provide smart task notifications and reminders.
  • To enable AI conversations based on your health data.
  • To help improve your wellness journey through daily tracking.

3. Data Storage and Security

All user data is securely stored using Firebase Firestore and Firebase Authentication. We follow industry best practices to protect your data from unauthorized access.

4. AI Services (Gemini API)

We use Gemini Flash API to offer personalized AI chat experiences. The content you share is used to generate responses and is not stored permanently on our servers.

5. Notifications

WellMate uses smart notifications to help you stay on track with your wellness goals and daily streaks. You can manage notification permissions through your device settings.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as Firebase, Google Gemini API, and analytics tools. These services may collect limited anonymized information to function properly.

7. Your Rights

  • You can request to view, update, or delete your data at any time.
  • You may delete your account via the app settings, which will remove all stored data.

8. Children's Privacy

WellMate is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. Users will be notified in-app of any major changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy, contact us at: support@wellmate.app
