About WellMate

Well OS is your smart, friendly wellness partner built to help you feel better every day — mentally, physically, and emotionally. Whether you're tracking your sleep, counting your steps, or chatting with your AI friend, Well OS is here to guide and support you like a true companion.

Created with love for students, dreamers, and everyday heroes who want to take care of their health while enjoying the journey — not stressing over it.

Well OS helps you build a better lifestyle with:

  • 😴 Automatic Sleep and Step Tracking
  • 🍔 Food Logging with calories and macros
  • 🔥 Calorie Burn estimation
  • 🧠 Mood & Mental Indicators (Focus, Calmness, Stamina)
  • 📈 Daily Progress Graphs
  • 🎯 Goal-based Missions & Streaks
  • 🤖 AI Chat Friend using Gemini Flash
  • 🔔 Friendly Smart Notifications
  • ✨ Clean and anime-inspired UI

Well OS doesn’t judge, doesn’t stress you — it just helps you improve, one small step at a time.
